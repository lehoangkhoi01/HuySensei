Ebi Sushi

Ebi sushi is tiger shrimp that is boiled, then frozen is iced water, and eventually butterflied and served either plain or with nigiri. The term ebi, however, has a broad meaning and refers to a variety of sushi preparation methods. In some cases the ebi dishes are served raw.
